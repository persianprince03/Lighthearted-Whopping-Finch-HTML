What is Intelag WFM?

Intelag WFM is a platform designed for hotel workforce management that utilizes AI-powered scheduling, integrates with employee apps, and optimizes guest experiences.

How can Intelag WFM benefit my hotel?

Intelag WFM can help optimize workforce efficiency, streamline scheduling processes, improve employee communication through app integration, and enhance guest satisfaction by ensuring adequate staffing levels.

What pricing plans does Intelag WFM offer?

Intelag WFM offers flexible pricing plans tailored to the specific needs of your hotel. Contact our sales team to discuss pricing options that best suit your requirements.

How does Intelag WFM use AI for scheduling?

Intelag WFM leverages AI algorithms to analyze historical data, forecast demand, and generate optimized schedules that take into account factors such as employee availability, skills, and labor laws.

Is Intelag WFM suitable for all sizes of hotels?

Yes, Intelag WFM is designed to cater to hotels of all sizes, from boutique establishments to large chains. The platform can be customized to meet the unique workforce management needs of each hotel.
