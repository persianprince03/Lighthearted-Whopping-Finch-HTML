About Us

Founded by Arman Dabiri, Intelag was created to solve the frustrating behind-the-scenes challenges that many hotels face—disconnected systems, miscommunications, and operational chaos. Built specifically for independent hotels, boutique resorts, and multi-property operators, Intelag delivers an all-in-one, cloud-based platform that streamlines workforce scheduling, guest messaging, front desk operations, housekeeping coordination, and real-time analytics. After witnessing how outdated tools and manual processes burdened hospitality teams and hurt guest satisfaction, Arman set out to build a smarter, more efficient solution. The result is a platform that empowers staff, simplifies management, and helps properties run more profitably and sustainably. Intelag: intelligent and automated globe solutions; intelligent and greener solutions. With intuitive design, mobile accessibility, and a commitment to innovation, Intelag isn’t just hotel software—it’s a smarter way to operate.
